Ramen

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Broth (chicken, pork, or vegetable)
  • Soy sauce or miso
  • Boiled egg
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Prepare the broth
  3. Add soy sauce or miso to the broth
  4. Place noodles in a bowl and pour the broth
  5. Top with egg and green onions
