Description

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Fresh fish (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Cucumber
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the sushi rice
  2. Place the rice on the nori sheet
  3. Add fish and vegetables
  4. Roll tightly with a bamboo mat
  5. Slice and serve with soy sauce
